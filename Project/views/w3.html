Book Your Metro Tickets

Quick and easy ticket booking for your metro rail journey. Choose your route, select your time, and travel with confidence.

How to Book Your Tickets:

1
Select your departure and destination stations from the route map
2
Choose your preferred travel date and time slot
3
Select the number of passengers and ticket type (adult/child/senior)
4
Review your booking details and total fare amount
5
Complete payment using your preferred method (card/wallet/UPI)
6
Receive your digital ticket via email and SMS confirmation
