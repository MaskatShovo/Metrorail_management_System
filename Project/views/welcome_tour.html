Welcome to MetroRail! 🚊

Get ready to explore the amazing features of our Metro Rail Management System. This quick tour will show you how to make the most of your journey with us.

🎯

Creating Your Account 📝

Learn how to create your account with just a few simple steps. All you need is your name, national ID, email, and a secure password.

👤

Booking Your Tickets 🎫

Discover how easy it is to book tickets! Choose your stations, select travel dates, pick your class, and you're ready to go. One-way or return - we've got you covered.

🎟️

Managing Your Bookings 📱

View your ticket history, download receipts, cancel bookings, and request refunds - all from your personal dashboard. Stay in control of your travel plans.

📋

Checking Train Schedules 🕒

Stay updated with real-time train schedules, departure times, and route information. Never miss your train with our comprehensive schedule system.

🚇

Additional Features ⚡

Explore fare calculator, time calculator, lost & found reporting, feedback system, and many more features designed to make your metro experience seamless and enjoyable.
